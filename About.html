About Us

We Are An Online Platform Dedicated To The Fight For Ocean Conservancy In Cameroon

Aquasafe Cameroon is an initiative for marine conservation by N. Mercy-Ruth E. This website is solely for educational and digital sensitization purposes. At Aquasafe Cameroon, our goal is to educate and inspire all individuals on the beauty and wonders that lie beneath the ocean. As Dr. Kamla, a prominent marine conservationist in Cameroon once said, "Giving them a glimpse of what lies beneath the ocean has sparked their love and willingness to protect the species in their waters". This quote emphasizes the importance of immersive experiences through captivating photos and videos in fostering a deeper connection between people and the Ocean. For truly, there is more to the ocean than we can imagine.
